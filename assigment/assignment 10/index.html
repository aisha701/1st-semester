THE NATURAL SCIENCE

Defination:

The natural sciences seek to understand how the world and universe around us works.

Branches of natural science

There are 3 main branches of Natural Science.
  1. Biology
  2. Chemistry
  3. Physics
Biology:

The study of living organisms, divided into many specialized fields that cover their morphology, physiology, anatomy, behaviour, origin, and distribution.

Chemistry:

The branch of science concerned with the substances of which matter is composed, the investigation of their properties and reactions, and the use of such reactions to form new substances.

Physics:

The branch of science concerned with the nature and properties of matter and energy. The subject matter of physics includes mechanics, heat, light and other radiation, sound, electricity, magnetism, and the structure of atoms.
