And Then There Were None

"And Then There Were None" is a mystery novel written by Agatha Christie and first published in 1939. It is the story of ten individuals who find themselves trapped on an isolated island. Each of them has received an invitation from an unknown host to visit the island for various reasons.

"Sapiens: A Brief History of Humankind" is a captivating non-fiction book written by Yuval Noah Harari.it presents a comprehensive overview of human history, tracing the development of Homo sapiens from its early origins to the present dayWhile "Sapiens" does not have a traditional plot or characters.
